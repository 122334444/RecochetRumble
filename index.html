Description

> The aim of game is to destroy opponent's Titan

# Pieces & their functions

  • Titan : The main piece to Protect & Destroy the opponent's titan to win the game.
  • Tank : Blocks bullets and moves one tile or rotates.
  • Ricochet : Reflects the bullets diagonally.
  • Semi-Ricochet : Reflects the bullet from its inclined surface and didappears when bullet hits the non reflecting surface.
  • Cannon : Fires bullets vertically & can move horizontally.
